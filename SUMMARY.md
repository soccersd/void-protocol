- VOID-PROTOCOL has no smart contracts
- Frontend-only project
- ✅ All Rights Reserved is correct

### **2. Why Public Repository?**
- Judges need to review code
- Competition requirement
- Transparency and trust
- ✅ Must be PUBLIC

### **3. Why TBD for All Specs?**
- Competition rules: "List TBD specs for power draw, noise, and pricing"
- Shows development is ongoing
- Professional approach
- Doesn't commit to specific numbers
- ✅ Required by rules

- ✅ Recommended character concept

- Automatic HTTPS
- Better performance
